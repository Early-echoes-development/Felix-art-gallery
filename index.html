Whether you are new to the art world or open to exploring the well known works of art from a new perspective the Art Conversations are designed to give you an unforgettable experience of fine art beyond the usual paths and the usual speed of everyday life.

Felix art conversations.
Tofa Yong, Artist.
Peter Mulder, Artist-Painter.

They are your step towards making connections with the fine art on a most personal level. You will slow down, you will disconnect from the daily buzz and you will discover how to truly dive in and enjoy the works of art of your choice.

Highly personalised, highly professional, unique and exclusively for you on spot tailor made experiences of taking it easy on masterpieces of fine art, MN Art Conversations are created by an art historian, led by an international fine art expert and a highly skilled radio host, dedicated to give you guaranteed the best experience you have ever had standing in front of a piece of the finest world renowned art.

Book today, we are looking forward to meet you!
